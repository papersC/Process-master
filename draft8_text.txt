Managing Customer Expectations
CONTEXT
Dubai National Housing Communities
KEY CHALLENGES
Eligibility Gap
Customers often expect eligibility 
despite not meeting policy criteria, 
leading to dissatisfaction.
Financial Impact
Frustration arising from unexpected 
differential amounts or outstanding 
arrears.
Exemption Hopes
Misconception that MBRHE will 
automatically cover costs or grant 
waivers upon request.
MBRHE RESPONSE STRATEGY
PROACTIVE COMMUNICATION
Clear online criteria & pre-eligibility checkers to 
manage expectations upfront.
FLEXIBLE TERMS
Structured repayment plans & hardship 
considerations for settlements.
EARLY ADVISORY
Guiding ineligible customers to alternative support 
channels immediately.
SERVICE ASSURANCE
Active case management with regular SMS 
updates and callbacks.
FINANCIAL TRANSPARENCY
Detailed cost breakdowns and counseling on 
financial obligations.
STAKEHOLDER COORDINATION
Direct links with finance partners to streamline 
valid approvals.
